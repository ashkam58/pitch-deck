04

5-Day Learning Journey

Day 1: AI Foundations & Machine Learning

Discover how AI works through Google's Teachable Machine. Train your first AI model!

Day 2: Conversational AI & Prompt Engineering

Master the art of AI dialogue with ChatGPT. Create stories, solve problems, and explore ideas.

Day 3: Creative Content Generation

Produce art, music, and comics using DALL·E, Boomy, and Canva. Unleash your creativity!

Day 4: Building AI Applications

Design games and chatbots using Scratch ML. No coding experience required!

Day 5: Ethics & Showcase

Present your AI creations and discuss responsible AI use. Become an AI advocate!
